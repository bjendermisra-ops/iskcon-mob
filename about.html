Our Mission

ISKCON (International Society for Krishna Consciousness), also known as the Hare Krishna movement, is a spiritual society founded to propagate the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita and Srimad Bhagavatam.

Our Hadapsar center is dedicated to spreading spiritual peace, Harinam Sankirtan, and Vedic education in society. We invite everyone to experience the pure bliss of devotion, beautiful Darshan, and Karma-free Prasadam.
